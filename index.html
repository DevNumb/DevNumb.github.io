What We Do at MEBCO

Manufacturing Excellence

MEBCO specializes in manufacturing automotive sealed maintenance-free batteries for all vehicle types. Our advanced technology plant represents the pinnacle of battery manufacturing in the region.

Product Range

We produce batteries for automotive applications, marine vessels, solar PV systems, UPS backup power, and deep cycle requirements. All products are manufactured to the highest quality standards under the trusted ACDelco brand.

Market Leadership

As the largest lead-acid battery manufacturer in the Arab world, we serve major automotive manufacturers and supply markets across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia with reliable energy storage solutions.

Economic Partnership

Established in 1995 as part of an economic offset program, MEBCO represents a successful strategic partnership between international automotive leaders and prominent Saudi business groups.

Innovation & Technology

Our facility utilizes the latest battery manufacturing technology, ensuring consistent quality, reliability, and performance. We continuously invest in innovation to meet evolving energy storage needs.

Quality Commitment

Every battery that leaves our facility meets stringent quality standards. We're committed to providing power solutions that automotive manufacturers and consumers can trust for long-lasting performance.
