What We Do at MEBCO

🔧 Manufacturing Excellence

MEBCO operates the largest and most advanced lead-acid battery manufacturing plant in the Arab world. We specialize in automotive sealed maintenance-free batteries using state-of-the-art stamped grid technology and rigorous quality control processes.

📦 Comprehensive Product Range

We produce batteries for automotive (American, Asian, European vehicles), marine, solar PV, UPS, heavy-duty, and deep cycle applications. All products are manufactured under the trusted ACDelco brand with exceptional quality standards.

🌍 Market Leadership

As the largest manufacturer in the region, we supply major automotive brands including GM, Ford, Chrysler, Toyota, Nissan, Fiat, Honda, and Mazda. Our distribution spans the entire GCC, Middle East, and international markets.

🤝 Strategic Partnership

Established in 1995 through an economic offset program, MEBCO represents a successful joint venture between Clarios (world's leading battery manufacturer) and prominent Saudi business groups, combining global expertise with local knowledge.

💡 Innovation & Technology

Our facility features the latest battery production technology with two decades of manufacturing expertise. We continuously invest in innovation to produce batteries that excel in the challenging Middle Eastern climate.

✅ Quality Commitment

Every MEBCO battery meets stringent international quality standards. We maintain ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO TS 16949, and OHSAS 18001 certifications, ensuring environmental responsibility and product excellence.
